I have recently been fiddling with some slot machine probability calculations and thought it would be fun to build a little web app to help visualize and experiment with different scenarios.

This ended up being a mix of an idle 'cookie clicker' style game mixed with a slot machine with a coding aesthetic. I thought it was fun and my son's middle school buddies got a kick out of it.

It is built with Pixi.js and is available at <https://slots.keathley.co>.
